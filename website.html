Pink City Prints

Inspired by a love of colour, craft and detail, Pink City Prints is dedicated to preserving ancient craft techniques which have been passed from generation to generation. Designed in London and handmade in India, each Pink City Prints dress combines traditional techniques such as block-printing and hand-embroidery with flattering and comfortable shapes made to be worn again and again.

Our Story
SUMMER 23
Shop Now

Our Summer 23 collection is a joyful celebration of craft, filled with pretty print mixes, sunshine ginghams and show-stopping cross stitch embroideries. From the irresistible Very Cherry block print to the punchy Clementine Rose, our new prints are colourful and fun, finished with playful ricrac in contrasting colours. Vintage details are scattered throughout the collection; there’s the pretty lace-trimmed Meryl dress, the hand-embroidered Goa dress and the scallop-trimmed Acapulco dress. Whether you’re heading to the beach in your Margot Mini, or dressing to impress in the Cross Stitch Amour Dress, this collection has something for everyone. We hope you love it as much as we do!
